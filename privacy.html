Privacy Policy – DUNE Delight Dakhla

1. Introduction

At DUNE Delight – Kite Guest House, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide when booking or contacting us.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal details such as name, email, phone number, and address
  • Booking information including room type, dates, and preferences
  • Passport or ID details as required by Moroccan law
  • Any messages, requests, or feedback you submit

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and confirm your bookings
  • To comply with Moroccan guest registration requirements
  • To communicate with you about your stay
  • To maintain safety, security, and legal compliance

4. Legal Basis

We process your data based on your consent (by submitting a booking) and our legitimate interest in managing reservations and complying with the law.

5. Data Protection & Security

All personal data and documents are securely stored and only used for booking purposes. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

6. Sharing Information

  • Guest information may be shared with Moroccan authorities if required by law
  • We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes

7. Data Retention

Booking data is retained for up to 12 months after your stay. Passport or ID documents are deleted once verification is complete.

8. Your Rights

  • You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at dunedelightdakhla@gmail.com
  • You can withdraw consent for data processing at any time for future bookings

9. Cookies & External Services

This site does not use tracking cookies. We may embed Google Maps for location purposes, which is subject to Google’s Privacy Policy.

10. Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected here with the revised “Last updated” date.
