1. Scope of Agreement

These Terms and Conditions apply to all bookings for accommodation at Dune Delight, whether for individual rooms or the entire house. By confirming a booking, the guest accepts these terms as binding.

2. Booking and Payment

A booking is considered confirmed once written confirmation is received and payment has been made according to the terms below.

Accepted payment methods:

  • Bank transfer: +3.6% processing fee for non-Moroccan accounts
  • PayPal: +2.9% processing fee
  • Revolut: +1.6% processing fee
  • Cash: accepted upon arrival

All payments must be completed before arrival, unless payment in cash has been agreed upon in advance. In that case, the full amount is due upon arrival at Dune Delight.

Bookings are only guaranteed once payment has been received in full or written confirmation of a cash payment arrangement has been provided.

3. Check-in and Check-out

  • Late check-out is subject to prior approval and may incur additional charges.

  • Exceeding the occupancy limits without prior approval is prohibited.
